// FAQ

Common Questions

Does my prop firm allow trade copying?
Most prop firms actively encourage it — when traders copy across multiple accounts, firms earn more in fees. Apex allows up to 20 accounts, Leeloo allows 3 Foundation accounts, and most others have similar policies. Always check your specific firm's terms.
Do I need NinjaTrader or Sierra Chart to use OrderClone?
No. That's the entire point. OrderClone connects directly at the API level — no trading platform required. You can trade on whatever platform you want (or none at all) and OrderClone handles the copying independently.
What is Sandbox Mode?
Sandbox Mode lets you test your entire group setup — sizing logic, instrument mapping, kill switches — with internal order routing that never touches live servers. Think of it as a dress rehearsal for your trade copying configuration.
How does smart instrument mapping work?
If your leader trades 1 ES contract and a follower is set to 20%, OrderClone automatically converts that to 2 MES contracts (the micro equivalent). It handles ES↔MES and NQ↔MNQ conversions on the fly based on your percentage-based sizing rules.
What happens when the early bird offer ends?
Your price locks in for life at 35% off. If you sign up during the early bird period, you'll never pay full price — even if regular pricing goes up later. Cancel and resubscribe after the offer? You'll pay the regular rate.
Is my data secure?
Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely. OrderClone only uses your login information to establish API connections on your behalf — we never place trades that you don't initiate from your leader account.
What's the "Zero Lock-In" about?
Other trade copiers force you into one platform (NinjaTrader) or one data provider (Rithmic only). OrderClone is platform and data provider independent — you're never locked into any single ecosystem. Switch firms, switch platforms, switch feeds. OrderClone keeps working.
